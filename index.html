Our Story

A Legacy of Taste Since 1989

Located in the heart of Rajajinagar, MAYURA 1989 Bar & Kitchen is more than just a restaurant; it's a destination for food connoisseurs. We bring you the bold, fiery flavours of Andhra cuisine, complemented by North Indian and Chinese specialities.

Our elegant dining area offers a breathtaking view of Bengaluru, making it the perfect spot for a romantic dinner or a lively evening with friends at our premium lounge.
