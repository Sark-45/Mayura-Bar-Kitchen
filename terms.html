Terms of Service

Reservations

We accept reservations for lunch, dinner, and brunch. Reservations are highly recommended for peak times to ensure your preferred seating.

Our venue can be booked for private events, including corporate gatherings and cocktail parties. Professional bar services are available on-site.

Dining Services

Options include dine-in, takeout, and delivery. We offer "all you can eat" selections and a dedicated premium bar menu.

We accept Credit/Debit cards, Google Pay, NFC mobile payments, and business checks for corporate events.

Our establishment is open daily from 11:00 AM to 11:30 PM, serving you throughout the week.

Experience our signature rooftop seating. Our venue features wheelchair-accessible seating and is family-friendly with high chairs available.
